🔐 Privacy Policy

I believe you have a right to know exactly what data Poetic Bytes collects, I collect it, and what I do with it. This policy is written in plain language — not legal boilerplate designed to obscure.

Last updated: May 2026

The Short Version

If you only read one section, read this. These are the key facts about privacy on Poetic Bytes.

🚫

I do not sell your data

Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Full stop.

📊

I use analytics to improve the site

I collect anonymised usage data (page views, session duration, country) to understand which lessons are helpful and where learners get stuck. This data is aggregated — not linked to you personally.

📢

I show ads via Google AdSense

Poetic Bytes is free because it is supported by advertising. Google AdSense may set cookies and show personalised or contextual ads based on your browsing behaviour. You can opt out via your Google account settings.

✉️

Newsletter is opt-in only

If you subscribe to our newsletter, I store your email address to send you Python lessons and updates. You can unsubscribe at any time with one click. We never add you without your explicit consent.

🌍

This site is based in the Philippines

Poetic Bytes is operated by Glenn Junsay Pansensoy from Diplahan, Zamboanga Sibugay, Philippines. Data is processed in accordance with the Philippines Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and applicable international standards.

📩

You can ask us to delete your data

If you have submitted a comment or subscribed to the newsletter, you can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will process your request within 30 days.

Poetic Bytes is a free Python learning platform created and operated by Glenn Junsay Pansensoy, an independent developer and writer based in Diplahan, Zamboanga Sibugay, Philippines. The platform is accessible at poetic-bytes.vercel.app and mirror deployments on GitHub Pages and Cloudflare Pages.

For the purposes of applicable data protection law, Glenn Junsay Pansensoy is the data controller — the person responsible for deciding how and why your personal data is processed. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, you can reach us through the contact page.

3. How I Use Your Data

I use your data only for the purposes described below. I do not use it for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

Providing and improving the service

Anonymised usage data tells me which Python lessons have the highest engagement, where learners typically drop off in a module, which devices and screen sizes need better support, and which content topics to prioritise in future updates. This is the primary use of automatically collected data.

Sending the newsletter

If you subscribe, I use your email address to send periodic Python lessons, site updates, and new content notifications. The legal basis for this processing is your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

Displaying and moderating comments

If you submit a comment, your display name and comment text are published on the relevant lesson page. We may moderate comments to remove spam or content that violates our terms. The legal basis is your explicit consent at time of submission.

Security and abuse prevention

IP addresses and request logs may be used to detect and block malicious traffic, scraping, denial-of-service attempts, or repeated abusive form submissions. Logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days for this purpose.

Advertising

Google AdSense uses cookies and your browsing data (subject to your consent and Google's own privacy settings) to serve relevant advertisements. This is how Poetic Bytes funds its operation as a free resource. The data used for ad personalisation is processed by Google, not by us directly.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. Poetic Bytes uses the following categories of cookies.

CategorySet byPurposeOpt-out
EssentialVercel / CloudflareLoad balancing, security, edge cachingCannot be disabled without breaking the site
AnalyticsAnalytics providerAnonymised session tracking to improve contentVia cookie preferences or browser settings
AdvertisingGoogle AdSenseAd personalisation and frequency cappingGoogle Ad Settings or browser opt-out extension

Poetic Bytes itself does not set first-party cookies beyond what is strictly necessary to serve the site. All persistent tracking cookies are set by third-party services (Google). For full details of the cookies used, see our Cookie Policy.

How to manage cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies on a per-site basis. Blocking advertising cookies will not prevent you from accessing any content on Poetic Bytes — all lessons remain freely available. It will result in generic, non-personalised ads being shown instead.

Deleting cookies

If you delete cookies, any saved preferences (such as theme settings or recently visited pages) stored in your browser will also be cleared. You may need to set them again on your next visit.

7. Your Rights

Under the Philippines Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and broadly aligned with GDPR principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal data.

👁️

Right to be informed

You have the right to know that your data is being collected, what data is collected, and how it is used. This privacy policy fulfils that obligation.

📋

Right of access

You can request a copy of the personal data I hold about you. I will respond within 30 days. For newsletter subscribers, this includes your email address and subscription date.

✏️

Right to rectification

If I hold inaccurate personal data about you, you can request that I correct it. For example, if your email address changes and you want your newsletter subscription updated.

🗑️

Right to erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time. For newsletter subscribers, the unsubscribe link in each email exercises this right immediately. For comments or other data, contact us directly.

🚫

Right to object

You can object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes (newsletter) at any time by unsubscribing. You can also object to analytics tracking by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

📦

Right to data portability

You can request your personal data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV). This applies to data you directly provided, such as your email subscription record.

How to exercise your rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact page. I will verify your identity and respond within 30 days. There is no charge for exercising your rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Poetic Bytes is an educational resource intended for learners of all ages, including students. However, I do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.

The newsletter subscription and comment features require users to be at least 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has submitted personal data to Poetic Bytes without your consent, please contact me immediately and I will delete the data promptly.

Learners under 13 are welcome to use all freely available lesson content on Poetic Bytes — no account, subscription, or personal data submission is required to access any Python tutorial on the site.

9. Security

I take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the personal data we hold against accidental loss, unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

Measures in place

All traffic between your browser and Poetic Bytes is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS 1.2 minimum, TLS 1.3 preferred). Our hosting providers — Vercel and Cloudflare — maintain enterprise-grade infrastructure security including DDoS protection, automated vulnerability patching, and physical data centre security. Newsletter subscriber data is stored with an industry-standard email provider using encrypted storage.

Limitations

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you discover a security vulnerability affecting Poetic Bytes, please disclose it responsibly by contacting us directly rather than posting it publicly.

Data breach notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with our obligations under RA 10173 and where applicable, GDPR.

10. Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in my practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When I make material changes, I will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

For significant changes — such as a new category of data collection or a new third-party service being added — we will also notify newsletter subscribers by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect, giving you the opportunity to unsubscribe if you disagree with the new terms.

Your continued use of Poetic Bytes after a policy update constitutes your acknowledgment of the revised policy. I encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests relating to this privacy policy or your personal data, I want to hear from you. I aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 working days.

Privacy enquiry or data request?

Use the contact page to get in touch. Please include "Privacy" in your subject line so we can route your message to the right place.
