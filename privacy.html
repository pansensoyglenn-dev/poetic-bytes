🔒 Data Privacy Policy

How Poetic Bytes collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information — and the choices you have over your data.

Effective: June 1, 2026 Last updated: June 2026 Philippines (RA 10173)

Plain-language summary

Poetic Bytes is a free Python learning platform. I do not sell your data. I collect only what is necessary to operate the site, serve relevant ads through Google AdSense, and improve content. You may request deletion of any personal information I hold by emailing us directly.

01

Who I Am

Poetic Bytes is an independent Python learning platform operated by Glenn Junsay Pansensoy, an individual developer and educator based in Diplahan, Zamboanga Sibugay, Philippines.

For the purposes of applicable data protection law (including the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, Republic Act No. 10173), Glenn Junsay Pansensoy is the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

  • Website: poetic-bytes.vercel.app
  • Contact: See the Contact section below
  • Social: Facebook · LinkedIn
02

Data I Collect

I collect data in two ways: information you actively provide to us, and information collected automatically when you use the site.

Information You Provide

  • Newsletter subscriptions — your email address when you choose to subscribe to the weekly Python digest.
  • Comments — your display name and comment content if you post in the Learner Voices section.
  • Contact messages — name, email address, and message content when you contact me for support.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on page, scroll depth, and referring URL, collected via Vercel Analytics.
  • Device & browser data — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preference.
  • IP address — used to determine approximate geographic region (country/city level) for analytics and ad serving. Not stored in a personally identifiable way beyond what third-party processors retain.
  • Cookies & local storage — see the Cookies section for full details.

Data I Do Not Collect

  • I do not collect payment information — Poetic Bytes is entirely free.
  • I do not require account registration or collect passwords.
  • I do not collect government-issued ID numbers, financial information, or biometric data.
03

How I Use Your Data

I use the data I collect for the following purposes, each of which has a lawful basis under applicable law:

  • Delivering the service — serving lesson pages, code examples, and curriculum content to your browser.
  • Newsletter delivery — sending you the weekly Python digest email you subscribed to. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.
  • Site analytics — understanding which lessons are most useful, how learners navigate the curriculum, and where content can be improved.
  • Advertising — displaying contextual ads via Google AdSense to fund the site's operation. Ad personalisation is subject to your cookie preferences and Google's own privacy policy.
  • Security & abuse prevention — detecting and blocking spam, bot traffic, and malicious requests.
  • Legal compliance — retaining records as required by Philippine law or responding to lawful requests from public authorities.

I do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

04

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. I use the following categories:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

  • These are required for the site to function. They do not store personally identifiable information. They cannot be disabled without breaking the site.

Analytics Cookies

  • Vercel Analytics — collects anonymous page view and performance data. No personally identifiable information is stored by this service.

Advertising Cookies

  • Google AdSense — sets cookies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing activity. Google's advertising cookies are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads or via the NAI opt-out tool.

Managing Cookies

  • You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.
  • For a full list of cookie controls by browser, visit allaboutcookies.org.
05

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

Poetic Bytes is a free resource funded by advertising revenue through Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-4575646748966866). Ads help cover hosting, domain, and development costs.

  • Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • I do not receive or store any data from Google that can identify you personally.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to my website or other websites.

To learn more, review Google's Advertising Policy. To opt out of personalised advertising, visit Google Ad Settings.

06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. I may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — Vercel (hosting and analytics), Google (AdSense and analytics), and email infrastructure providers. All are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — If required by Philippine law, court order, or other governmental authority, I may disclose data to comply with a legal obligation.
  • Safety — To protect the rights, property, or safety of Poetic Bytes, its users, or the public.
  • Business transfer — In the unlikely event that the site is sold or transferred, user data would transfer to the new owner subject to equivalent privacy protections.

Third-Party Links

Poetic Bytes contains links to external sites (GitHub, LinkedIn, Stack Overflow, etc.). I have no control over the content or privacy practices of those sites and are not responsible for their policies. Please review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

07

Data Retention

I retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Newsletter email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe. Unsubscription removes your email from all future sends within 10 business days.
  • Comments — retained indefinitely unless you request removal. I may remove comments that violate my community standards at any time.
  • Contact messages — retained for up to 12 months from the date of receipt, then securely deleted.
  • Analytics data — anonymised aggregate analytics data may be retained indefinitely for trend analysis. No personally identifiable data is retained beyond 26 months.
  • Server logs — raw server access logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging, then automatically purged.
08

Security

I take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These include:

  • HTTPS (TLS) encryption for all data in transit between your browser and my servers.
  • Hosting on Vercel's infrastructure, which includes DDoS protection, edge security, and automatic security patches.
  • Minimal data collection — the best security for data you don't need is not to collect it.
  • Access to any stored personal data (e.g., newsletter subscriber lists) is restricted to the site operator only.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While I strive to protect your data, I cannot guarantee absolute security. If you become aware of a security vulnerability, please contact me immediately at the address in the Contact section.

09

Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), and where applicable under GDPR or other regional laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to be informed — You have the right to know what personal data I collect and how it is used. This policy fulfils that obligation.
  • Right of access — You may request a copy of the personal data I hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to my legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Right to object — You may object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes (including email newsletters) at any time.
  • Right to data portability — Where processing is based on your consent, you may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me using the details in the Contact section. I will respond within 30 days. I may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10

Children's Privacy

Poetic Bytes is intended for a general audience. I do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where required by applicable law).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us without your consent, please contact me immediately. I will take prompt steps to delete such information from my systems.

Where a user provides information suggesting they are a minor, I will process that information with additional care and in accordance with applicable child data protection requirements.

11

International Users

Poetic Bytes is operated from the Philippines and is used by learners in 50+ countries. By using this site, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in the Philippines or in countries where my service providers (Vercel, Google) operate data centres.

  • European users (GDPR) — I process your data on the legal basis of legitimate interests (for analytics and site operation) and consent (for newsletter subscription and personalised ads). You have the rights described in Section 9.
  • California users (CCPA) — I do not sell personal information. You have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. Contact me to exercise these rights.
  • Philippine users (RA 10173) — You are entitled to all rights described in Section 9. You may also file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph if you believe I have violated your data privacy rights.
12

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in my practices, technologies, or legal requirements. When I do:

  • The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
  • For material changes, I will post a notice on the Poetic Bytes homepage for at least 30 days.
  • If you have subscribed to my newsletter, I may notify you of significant changes by email.

Continued use of Poetic Bytes after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. If you do not agree with the updated terms, please discontinue use of the site and contact me to request deletion of any data I hold.
