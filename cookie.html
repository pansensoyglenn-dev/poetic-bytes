Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Poetic Bytes (hosted at https://pansensoyglenn-dev.github.io/poetic-bytes/) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies.

Because this is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not use cookies for authentication, personalization, analytics, or advertising in the conventional sense.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They help the site remember information about your visit (language preference, login state, analytics data, etc.).

3. Cookies used on this site

3.1 Strictly necessary / functional cookies

None. This site does not require cookies to function.

3.2 Performance / analytics cookies

None. We do not currently embed Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, Matomo, or any other third-party analytics script.

3.3 Third-party cookies

None by default. However, the following situations may cause third-party cookies to appear:

  • Embedded GitHub Gists or repositories (GitHub may set cookies)
  • Embedded X / Twitter, YouTube, or other social media widgets (if added in future)
  • Browser extensions or ad blockers that inject their own scripts

3.4 Session / preference cookies

None. No dark mode toggle, language switcher, or other client-side preferences are stored via cookies at this time.

4. How to manage cookies

Since this site does not set any first-party cookies, you generally do not need to take action. However, you can control cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

You can also use browser extensions such as uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or ClearURLs for more granular control.

5. Changes to this policy

If we begin using cookies or third-party services in the future (e.g., analytics, comment system), this page will be updated and the change date noted below.
