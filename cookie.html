How I Use Cookies on Poetic Bytes

This policy explains what cookies are, how I use them, the types of cookies I set, and how you can manage your preferences.

🍪 What Are Cookies?

Small text files that help me deliver a better experience

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve user experience, and provide useful information to website owners.

At Poetic Bytes, I use cookies to:

  • Enhance functionality — remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze usage — understand how visitors interact with my content
  • Improve performance — monitor site speed and reliability
  • Personalize content — tailor recommendations and learning paths
💡 By using my website, you consent to my use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. You can change your preferences at any time.
📅 Effective: June 21, 2026

📋 Types of Cookies I Use

Understanding the different categories of cookies on my site

🔒 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, security, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as they are strictly required for the service.

  • Session cookies — maintain your session state across pages
  • Security cookies — help protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF)
  • Load balancing cookies — distribute traffic across servers

📊 Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies help me understand how visitors use my site, which pages are most popular, and how I can improve the learning experience. All data is collected anonymously.

  • Google Analytics — tracks page views, time on site, and traffic sources
  • Performance monitoring — helps me identify technical issues
  • Usage patterns — which lessons and features are most used
💡 I use Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled. This means your IP address is truncated before being stored, making it impossible to identify you personally.

⚙️ Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to personalize your experience.

  • Theme preferences — remember your display settings
  • Code runner state — remember your code snippets and outputs
  • Comment settings — remember your display name for comments

📢 Marketing & Advertising Cookies

I use Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements. These cookies help me show ads that are more likely to be relevant to you and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

  • Ad personalization — shows ads based on your browsing history
  • Ad frequency — limits how often you see the same ad
  • Conversion tracking — measures ad performance
⚠️ You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.

🛠️ Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over which cookies you accept

🌐 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies — prevent any cookies from being set
  • Delete existing cookies — clear all cookies stored on your device
  • Block third-party cookies — accept only first-party cookies
  • Set cookie preferences per site — customize per website

For detailed instructions, visit the help section of your browser:

  • • Google Chrome
  • • Mozilla Firefox
  • • Apple Safari
  • • Microsoft Edge

📧 Opt Out of Analytics

You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting data about you by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on for your browser.

  • Download the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on
💡 Blocking cookies may affect the functionality of my site, including the interactive code runner and comment system.

🔗 Third-Party Cookies

Cookies set by external services I use

I use trusted third-party services that may also set cookies on your device. These services include:

  • Google Analytics — analytics and performance monitoring (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — display advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel / GitHub Pages — hosting and deployment (subject to their privacy policies)

I do not have direct control over these third-party cookies. I recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.
